Mental Health in Schools

A Media Activism Cause Website

Date

November-December 2016

Class

Media Activism

In the first year humanities class Media Revolution:Activism and Technology, we spent the semester studying the different ways in which media plays a role in modern day activism and communication of information. For our final project, we created a website to communicate information about a cause of our choice. Within this site we included identification of the problem, a link to a longer form essay on the topic, and a call to action. Within the call to action portion, we included a variety of options to reach different audiences. Some of these involved becoming informed, participating in discussion, or giving to groups that have more information to support the cause.

For my website I chose to focus on the issue of access to mental health resources in school. For me this had personal significance coming from the California Bay Area, an area notorious for high levels of stress and academic pressure. You can browse through my site here.
